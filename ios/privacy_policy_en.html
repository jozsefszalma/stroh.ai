Application Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2023

Introduction:

Welcome to our Application ("we", "us", or "our"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use our Application ("App") and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

Information We Collect:

The App collects the following types of information when you use it:

  • User ID: This unique identifier is provided by Apple through the 'Sign-in with Apple' functionality, enabling us to identify your account within our system.
  • Precise Location: We collect your precise location information to facilitate specific application functionalities.
  • Conversation History: We collect your conversation history to facilitate specific application functionalities.

Information We Do Not Collect:

We want to emphasize that we do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), unique device IDs, or use cookies or other tracking technologies.

Use of Your Information:

The information we collect is strictly used to facilitate specific application functionalities. We do not track our users or their activities beyond the scope of the App. Furthermore, we do not sell, trade, or distribute the collected data to third parties for marketing, advertising, or any purposes unrelated to the app's functionalities.

Data Transfer:

Please note that your data is transferred to the United States because our services utilize the OpenAI's GPT-4 API, which processes API requests within the US.

Sharing of Information:

Your location data and conversation history are shared with OpenAI due to our use of their API. You can review OpenAI's data usage policies at https://openai.com/policies/api-data-usage-policies.

Storage and Security of Information:

Both we and OpenAI store your data for a maximum duration of 30 days. During this period, your data is encrypted and securely stored to protect against unauthorized access. After this period, your data is automatically deleted. We also encrypt all data during transfer to ensure your data security.

Review of Information:

We only review your information in cases of investigations related to potential misuse or abuse of our services, or when addressing customer service requests. We do not utilize your data for any other purposes.

User Age Limit:

The application has an Age Rating of 17+ on the Apple App Store. We do not knowingly collect or maintain data from minors.

Remember, the right to amend our Privacy Policy is reserved at any time, and we recommend regular reviews. Your continued use of our App after any changes signifies your acceptance of those changes.

If you have any queries or concerns about this policy, please contact us at privacy@stroh.ai.
